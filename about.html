About Us

CV Java Agro Mandiri (JAVARI) has vision to be a resilient and robust company to collaborate with communities to develop rural commodities which convern to sustainable environment.

Javari or Java Agro Mandiri are youth community that has concerns related to development and poverty in the rural area. In our point of view, rural areas have a big potency to be developed so that the rural communities will be more prosperous. To answer those challenges, we build a social entrepreneur called CV Java Agro Mandiri (JAVARI) which focus on developing commodities to strengthen and support the rural livelihood.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be a robust company in business that having orientation on sustainable environment and increasing the prosperity of rural livelihood

Our Mision

  1. Increase capacity for business management and trusted supply chain
  2. Developing the added value for rural commodities that environmentally friendly
  3. Developing and expanding sustainable domestic and international market

Integrated Organic Projects

Our Integrated Organic Project is a sustainable farming initiative that promotes organic farming practices and supports local communities in Indonesia. We work with farmers to develop sustainable agriculture systems that are environmentally friendly and socially responsible. Our goal is to create a better future for farmers and their families by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed.

Internal Control System

Our Internal Control System (ICS) is a set of policies and procedures that are designed to ensure that our products are produced in a safe and environmentally friendly manner. We have implemented a number of measures to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and safety. Our ICS includes regular inspections of our facilities, testing of our products, and training of our employees. We are committed to providing our customers with the best coconut sugar products that are good for their health and the planet.
